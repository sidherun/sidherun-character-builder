## The Rohnar, the Southern Shores People, the Inner Sea, and the East-West Highway

The Southern Shores consists of an area stretching from southern tip of the Spine of the World Mountains, down around the East-West Highway, and North again around much of the Inner Sea. The area itself has several different microclimates, from very rainy to the West and Northwest to ridiculously hot and dry to the Southeast to very temperate around the northern circle of the Inner Sea. The people that inhabit this area have survived war and famine for the past two centuries as various tribes, armies and invaders took the land back and forth, as the precious farming land in the East-West Highway region is considered the most valuable land on the continent.

The Inner Sea presents the calmest salt-water port available to the far East of the area, controlled by the new Naval forces brought to the Southern Shores through both conquest and great wealth from the farmland the Rohnar's control. Several cities, towns and villages decorate the area, with the capital, Stardock, as the crucial point in the Kingdom. The current ruler, Thoraine Rohnar, son of the Great Conqueror, Vonaheimr Rohnar, has ruled this area for the past 50 years, in relative peace. The 'People' as they are called here, descend from a seafaring tribe who inhabited a large settlement along the Vast Ocean of the far West.

The People have always been an uncommon lot. They do not adhere to the common idea that beings from different races are superior (or inferior) to the rest. The current rulers, although human in descendent, have long counted allies (and in fact family) the Dwarves, Fae, Avian, and others into the People, if those groups have kept to the mantra of the region, 'As All, We Go Forward'.

Thoraine became ruler of the area prior to the founding of some of the largest and most powerful northern villages, cities, and towns, and all have seen great growth under his rulership. King's Town, Thoraine's home was a simple fishing village when Thoraine was elevated, and now is home to one of the most powerful harbors in the world, with over 400 boat and shipping slips available, and patrolled by the largest known naval fleet.

**Weather and Terrain**

The area of the Inner Sea itself features warm, humid summer months, cool winters, but mostly calm weather. The East-West highway area is similar, except for the western area, which is prone to receive great storms in the fall. Only a few days to the Northwest, however, and you'll ascend into the Spine, where temperatures in the winter can freeze a man to death in a few hours. You can also experience a quite different pattern heading due north, where the city of Quin'tiel marks the beginning of the Grove, home of many Fae creatures, and land of the Druids, the Protectors of Sidherun. Heading East into the Highlands, men of many tribes' wrestle for territory with other races, beasts and the unknown. As you head northeast, into the northern territories, there is little charted, and stories of horror persist throughout the lands, originating from the fell city of Oreg ar Nata

Recently, the eastern and southern coasts of the Inner Sea have seen a great deal of strife, as various sea-borne warlords look to gather forces and take villages from other strongmen. The areas outside the Southern Shores along the Inner Sea have long experienced strife, warfare, and piracy, and that continues, even as the Rohnar people grow and prosper.

**People and Politics**

Each city within the Southern Shores is ruled by two people:

- A Mayor, who oversees the day-to-day affairs of the city, and is appointed by the Royan Exchequer
- A Duke, who oversees the military, naval and Royal interests in the city and all surrounding towns.

In each city in Southern Shores, there are two groups that the citizens must learn to deal with (and pay respect to). All cities, most towns and some villages have a mayor, who is appointed by the Royan Exchequer, to run daily affairs. The mayor typically oversees the gathering of taxes, handles disputes in one of the many court systems that exist in the area, and is the approver for all business dealings for their area. In all cities but one, the mayor also oversees the City Guard, which handles enforcement of the Rules. The Rules are the common name of the group of Royal Decrees, general contract rules and whims of the royalty. Most of the Rules involve either taxes, civil law, or punishment. In the city of Stardock, the Duke of Stardock is also the mayor, and has an entourage of servants who run daily activities on his behalf.

For all the territory around the cities, the various Dukes, and Barons of the Court of Rohnar rule the area. The dukes and their men are accountable only to the King and act as the outright rulers of much of the territory of Southern Shores. King Thoraine has 4 sons and 3 daughters who all have Ducal or Baronial titles, and rule most of the Kingdom as it stands today. As the military leaders of the People, they uniformly see respect from the residents, either through love or fear, depending on the family member being discussed.
