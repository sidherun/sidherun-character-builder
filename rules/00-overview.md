# Story Driven
<!-- source: Sidherun PHB 2_8_2026.docx -->

Sidherun is a Role-playing game based on the concept of story-driven gaming. The rules of Sidherun exist to support the narrative being created by the Game Master (GM) and the players. At any point, the GM may decide to set aside any rule for any reason, which drives the narrative. This is typically done where the storyline, or actions of the players involved create a narrative superior to what is feasible within the rule set.

It's important to remember rule deviation is not taken lightly, and in all cases, the point of the change is to facilitate a superior story for the players and/or push forward the narrative the group is driving towards. At times, this action can cause some discomfort. In this case, it's ok to revisit the decision as a group and come to a resolution. Typically, this is done with an understanding of the big picture and taller narrative.
