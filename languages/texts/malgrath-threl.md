# Malgrath'threl — "The Scar Malgrath Carries" in Quin'lae

<!-- source: translated from the World Story "The Scar Malgrath Carries" (Sidherun Notion, World Stories); second Quin'lae text, 2026-07-18 -->

The second full text in Quin'lae, translated from the World Story "The Scar Malgrath Carries" as a compressed elven telling. Where Tem'wen stress-tested the v2.0 basics, this text works the v2.1–v2.2 machinery: base-12 numerals (stanzas 1, 5, 11, 16), ordinals, a conditional (stanza 4), relatives, and the antonym prefix. Grammar and lexicon per [`../quindel.md`](../quindel.md) §11–§12; the ~20 coinages this text added are logged there.

Two conventions this text establishes:

- **Foreign names stay foreign.** Vorrath, Malgrath, Grandanto, and Dûrakhar keep their native forms, phonotactics violations and all — that is how Quin'lae marks a loan (§9). The one common-noun loan is *khel* "iron", taken from the Dûrakhar with its alien k intact.
- **New divine names:** **Ith'ver** ("the Deep Green") is the Quin'lae name for Roughnira; **Seth'wen** ("the Still Wind") is the Quin'lae name for Serakhala.

Format per stanza: Quin'lae, then word-by-word gloss, then plain English.

---

1. **Elir sor'vor nel dinin. Davir Vorrath ansa: nor, wenil, slaen, ar vath.**
   burned oath-fire three days · gave Vorrath to-it: bone, feather, vine, and blood
   The oath-fire burned for three days. Vorrath fed it: bone, feather, vine, blood.

2. **Nu methir sa. Quera meth an hal, ar ea hal haldon.**
   not rested he · asks sleep for holding, and is holding a-cage
   He did not sleep. Sleep asks to be held, and to be held is a cage.

3. **Methir drion Malgrath im ven o sor'vor, alar ol drionin. Nu sethir sa'velilin.**
   rested wolf Malgrath at edge of oath-fire, older than wolves · not grew-still his-eyes
   The wolf Malgrath lay at the fire's edge, older than wolves are given to be. His eyes never stilled.

4. **«Im dineth,» vorir sa an drion, «runa nin im.» Nu vorir drion. Nu vora drionin, quen nu nara sain.**
   in the-morning, said he to the-wolf, walk we in · not spoke wolf · not speak wolves, if not need they
   "In the morning," he told the wolf, "we go in." The wolf said nothing. Wolves do not speak unless they must.

5. **Nu vorir Run'thrin o Ith'don Otheth. Im sa: el'hal Dûrakhar, orinilin oth, halen veth khel.**
   not spoke Thorn-Road of Deep-Place Seventh · in it: crown Dwarven, shards seven, held with iron
   The Thorned Path did not speak of the Seventh Vault. Inside it: a Dûrakhar crown in seven shards, sealed in iron.

6. **Vorir Grandanto enu sa'seth: «Vela min orinilin os ith. Tolir sa im enu halen, ar nu runir sa os.»**
   said Grandanto before his-stillness: seeks something the-shards from the-deep · came it in before the-sealing, and not walked it out
   Before his death Grandanto said: "Something in the deep is looking for the shards. It got in before the sealing, and it never found its way out."

7. **Vorir Vorrath ansa: «Meth.» Sethir Grandanto.**
   said Vorrath to-him: rest · grew-still Grandanto
   Vorrath told him to sleep. Grandanto died.

8. **Eir sa im tiel, veth Vel'orin soren im sa'taril. Nu hala min aia sa'vor.**
   stood he at the-gate, with Seeing-stone warm in his-hand · not holds one always one's-word
   He stood at the threshold, the Lens warm in his hand. No one keeps every promise.

9. **Im dineth runir sa im. Tolir drion; nu querir min ansa.**
   at morning walked he in · came wolf; not asked anyone him
   At dawn he went in. The wolf came without being called.

10. **Eir Ith'don nim, ar nu eir sa nim o orin. Eir sa nim o Seth'wen, ya nu vira, ya nu vera. Nuelir sa'threlin thrin. Nu meorir don min ver.**
    was Deep-Place cold, and not was it cold of stone · was it cold of Still-Wind, who not turns, who not grows · went-dark his-marks thorn · not remembered the-ground any life
    The vault was cold, and the cold was not the stone's. It was the cold of Serakhala the Stillness, who does not turn and does not grow. His thorn-marks dimmed. The ground remembered no living thing.

11. **Eir orinilin im donin oth, halen veth khel. Velir Vel'orin: eir wes soren, eir otheth nim.**
    were shards in places seven, held with iron · saw Seeing-stone: were six warm, was seventh cold
    The shards lay in seven alcoves, chained in iron. The Lens counted them: six warm, the seventh cold.

12. **Eir sa enu don laden maen wenin.**
    stood he before the-place open many breaths
    He stood before the empty alcove for many breaths.

13. **Vorir Malgrath vor al ya nu hala len.**
    spoke Malgrath a-sound old that not holds a-name
    Malgrath made an old sound that has no name.

14. **Runir min ter duth. Eir Malgrath sul orin. Nuelir Vel'orin. Imya runir sa os, eir threl im drion — threl ya nu eir enu.**
    walked something through the-shadow · was Malgrath like stone · went-dark Seeing-stone · when walked it away, was a-mark on the-wolf — a-mark that not was before
    Something passed in the dark. Malgrath stood like stone. The Lens went cold. When it had gone, there was a mark on the wolf that had not been there before.

15. **Nu eir vath, nu eir sor'vor, nu eir davenin. Eir threl min. Threl o Ith'ver.**
    not was blood, not was oath-fire, not was offerings · was mark alone · mark of Deep-Green
    No blood-oath, no fire, no offerings. Only a mark. Roughnira's mark.

16. **Davir Ith'ver sa'threl an drion, an velor — nu an Vorrath, ya davir ansa vath ar nor sil ar dol sorunin.**
    gave Deep-Green her-mark to the-wolf, to the-witness — not to Vorrath, who gave to-her blood and bone twelve-and-eight years
    Roughnira gave her mark to the wolf — to the witness — and not to Vorrath, who had given her blood and bone for twenty years.

17. **Nu vorir sa; ono meorir Vorrath sa'vor: «Nu haluin thu ya toluin. Ar meoruin sa.»**
    not spoke she; yet remembered Vorrath her-word: not will-hold you what will-come · and will-remember he
    She said nothing; yet Vorrath knew her meaning: "You will not hold what is coming. But he will remember."

18. **Runir sa os, taril im threl o drion. Nu threla Ith'ver ansa ya nuveluin sa; threla sa ansa ya veluin sa.**
    walked he out, hand on mark of the-wolf · not marks Deep-Green that-which will-forsake she; marks she that-which will-watch she
    He walked out with his hand on the wolf's scar. Roughnira does not mark what she means to forsake. She marks what she means to watch.
